Cristian Apolinar

Doctoral Candidate | Criminology, Law & Society | University of California, Irvine

I study proactive policing and its community-level consequences, with a focus on how place-based enforcement strategies shape neighborhood conditions and equity. My work combines longitudinal and spatial methods to evaluate policy impacts and produce findings that are interpretable for both scholarly and practitioner audiences.
