Cristian Apolinar

Doctoral Candidate | Criminology, Law & Society | University of California, Irvine

I conduct policy-relevant research on proactive policing and its downstream consequences for communities. Using longitudinal and spatial methods, I study how place-based enforcement strategies shape neighborhood conditions, public safety, and equity. I am especially interested in translating rigorous empirical work into findings that are useful to practitioners, policymakers, and community stakeholders.
