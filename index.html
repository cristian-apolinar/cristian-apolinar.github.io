Cristian Apolinar

Ph.D. Candidate, Criminology, Law & Society, University of California, Irvine (anticipated June 2026)

I conduct policy-relevant research on the collateral consequences of proactive policing strategies, with a focus on racial and ethnic disparities, community well-being, and police–community relations. I use quantitative and spatial methods, including geocoding and GIS workflows in Stata and ArcGIS Pro.
